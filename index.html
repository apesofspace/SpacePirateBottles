What is
«Space Pirate Bottles»?

Our hero loves adventures and is always ready for reckless schemes. For years he has been dreaming of wealth and fame, surfing space in search of the most valued treasures. Unsurprisingly, he is the one who decided to search for numerous pieces of a large puzzle. He was the first to realize they were part of an ancient map compiled many centuries ago by beings of a civilization.

What was hidden on the map? Coordinates of the place where the treasure was kept! In search of map fragments, he had to visit many star systems and each of its planets. Until one day, all the fragments of the lost map were in his hands!

The puzzle was completed, and at the same moment, our hero understood where the yearned-after treasures were. He managed to get to the secret location and find the treasure quickly. Since he was honest, generous, and grateful, he immediately decided to share the godsend with his best friends, but even after the closest ones were gifted, he still had many treasures left.

Our space pirate started to carry them from one planet to another, from one universe to another, lavishing those who were dear or just appealing to him. His enemies got wind of it soon. They were furious that our hero had become rich and launched a hunt for him.

It’s better to be safe than sorry, so our hero decided to get rid of the treasures. He split them into parts and hid them on different planets in his own and the other galaxy. There were so many hiding places that he had to make a list of them. At the very moment when the list was completed, an asteroid suddenly crashed into our hero’s ship – his enemies stage-managed it. All navigation systems immediately went out of order, and our pirate lost the ability to pilot the ship. He knew that he had to act. There were many dishes on the spaceship since he loved to receive guests and often threw parties.

To make everyone understand that he really needed help and this was not a deception, he put fragments of the treasure list in some of the bottles. Having sealed the bottles tightly, the pirate threw them into outer space, hoping that the astronauts would find them and help him.

At some point, the spacecraft managed to enter the atmosphere of a small but cozy planet, where our hero eventually took sanctuary from his enemies. His spacecraft was badly damaged during the collision, and he was unable to repair it on his own. But our space pirate did not sink into despair. He still believes that his messages will get into the hands of those who will come for him to the small planet and help him reunite with his friends.
